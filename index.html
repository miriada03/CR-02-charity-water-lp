Maria's Story: Clean Water Changes Everything

Before clean water came to her village in Ethiopia, 8-year-old Maria walked 3 hours every day to collect water from a distant pond. Now, with a new well just 5 minutes from her home, Maria attends school full-time and dreams of becoming a teacher.
